Water Park

ALL SLIDES ARE OPERATIONAL

All slides are operational, ready to make a splash! Dive into a day of fun and excitement as you enjoy every twist, turn, and drop in the water park.

THE JUNGLE FORTRESS

A thrilling aquatic adventure awaits, with roaring waves and tropical vibes, providing a perfect escape for water enthusiasts and families alike.

Hide & Seek SLide

Experience an adrenaline rush on the Hide & Seek SLide ! Twist, turn, and speed down an exhilarating water ride that will leave you craving more excitement at the water park.

FUSION SLIDE

Unleash your inner thrill-seeker on the Tornado Slide! Brace for a whirlwind of excitement as you spiral down this heart-pounding water adventure. Are you ready for the ride?

WAVE POOL

A big attraction for the young and old alike is the Wave Pool- an artificial sea where huge waves are generated in three different patterns.If you have had enough, relax by the wave pool under thatched roof umbrellas and sip on a cool mock tail.

FAMILY SLIDES

Family slides, the perfect choice for an unforgettable day out! Share laughter and thrills with loved ones as you glide down these exhilarating slides, making cherished memories together.

FUN SLIDE

Prepare for the ultimate plunge on the Drop Slide! Experience an adrenaline rush like never before as you free-fall down a towering water slide. Are you daring enough?

WATER PARK

As a member of the World Water Park Association, USA. the Fun N Food water park AKA Fun N Food Village offers India’s largest collection of Water slides and attractions for toddler learning how to swim to the Water sports enthusiast seeking a thrill. Thrills’ chills and spills don’t miss them at the Fun n Food Water Park in Delhi offers its patrons a new twisting turning and sliding experience with the longest Water slide in the Country.

LAZY RIVER

Relax on the Lazy Slide. Drift gently down the meandering waterway, soaking in the sun and serene surroundings, the perfect way to unwind in the water park paradise.

TORNADO SLIDE

Experience the Torpedo Slide, a high-speed aquatic thrill ride! Plunge into the unknown, racing through twists and turns that will leave you breathless and eager for more.

DROP SLIDE

Get ready for a chilling adventure on the Blizzard Slide! Twist, turn, and swirl through a frozen vortex of excitement, a snowstorm of thrills awaits on this icy ride.

RAIN DANCE

Dance in the rain! Our Rain Dance area is a splash of joy and beats. Join the fun, let loose, and groove to music amidst refreshing water sprays.

SPEED COASTER

Endless fun on the Fun Slide! A thrilling and joyful ride for all ages. Twist, turn, and laugh your way down this exciting water slide adventure.

BLIZZARD Slide

Experience the Fusion Slide: A thrilling blend of twists, turns, and drops. Take on this exhilarating water ride that fuses excitement and adventure for an unforgettable aquatic journey.

Amusement Park

MAGIC CARPET

Come on a fantastical journey filled with amazing experiences. Allow the spinning wheel to take you to an unparalleled realm where you experience the sensation of soaring over the skies. Enjoy a dreamlike experience with each spin, and let your laughter scream out as you engage on this fantastical journey filled with happiness and adventure.

ROCK N ROLL

With the ultimate rollercoaster experience, get ready to rock and roll and bring your fun side. Take in the rush of excitement as you play through the most memorable riffs and powerful solos. Savor the twists and turns of the upbeat, adventurous and joyful sentiments. Experience a rock and roll voyage and let your hair fly in the ultimate waves of adventure and adrenaline.

MERRY GO ROUND

Come on a thrilling journey on a merry-go-round and feel the exhilarating waves of adventure. Hold on tight as we take you on an adventurous trip that will build memories while keeping you having fun on our merry-go-round. Every day is a fresh adventure, giving you the impression that you are plunging into the water or being on cloud nine. Join us for a fun and exciting trip where you may let your imagination run wild with every turn.

WATER QUEEN

Take a spectacular experience on the Water Queen ride, and indulge in the adventure to let go of all your worries and tension by bringing your inner child. Immerse yourself in the autumn of adventure as you experience the glistening streams and tumbling waterfalls. Take to the waves of wonder and sail away with the Water Queen.

Tora Tora

Tora Tora will have your heart racing as it launches you into an exhilarating whirlwind. Fasten your seatbelt and experience the surge of excitement coursing through your veins as you soar over loops and bends. This ride promises to be an incredible experience with of breath-taking drops and gravity-defying feats that will leave you wanting more.

JUNGLE CAROUSEL

Enter the exotic realm of Jungle Carousel, where you will be able to enjoy an incredible level of adventure. Take a ride with colorful jungle animals, such as roaring lions and swinging monkeys, and let the rhythmic music transport you throughout the entire experience. The Jungle Carousel offers adventurers of all ages a magical journey where the magic of the jungle is revealed with each spin in a whirlwind of delight and amazement.

MINI PIRATE SHIP

Take a trip on our MINI PRIVATE SHIP and feel what it's like to own a private ship, sailing it with all of the excitement and passion you can muster. As you move through blue seas, feel the soft swing of the waves beneath your feet. Enjoy the peace of your own haven while exploring uncharted territory and unexplored perspectives. Allow your tiny private ship to become the dreamboat that takes you to unimaginable places.

Mini Wheel

Come feel the thrills and love that each small carriage on our adorable Ferris wheel has to offer. Move with the flow of the soft breeze and experience the soft sway where memories are created and laughter permeates the atmosphere. Settle in, tighten your grip, and let the Mini Wheel transport you on an incredible voyage beyond your wildest imagination.

SEA HORSE TRAIN

For a fun ride along the edge of the water, board the Sea Horse Train. Saddle up in the vibrant carriages fashioned like regal marine life, and let the steady chug transport you past sandy beaches and breaking waves. Wind through secret coves and seaside villages while listening to the cry of seagulls. Take in the music of the sea and the salty wind with every swing. Join us on the Sea Horse Train, where each journey takes you into the fascinating world that lies beneath the sea.

FLYING CHAIR

Experience the refreshing mist caressing your face as it falls softly over you, providing an enjoyable experience. As you spin about with excitement and delight, uncover new wonders that provide you with an enjoyable experience. Allow pleasure to uplift your spirits and laughter to flood your heart as the chairs swing and dance in the air. As you progress through each turn, heighten your enthusiasm and turn every adventure into a treasure trove of exhilarating memories just waiting to be discovered.

JUNGLE BOUNCY

As you bounce around the jungle, you'll discover a thrilling world of adventure. Swirling laughter reverberates around you as you soar higher and higher, giving you a rush of excitement. Navigate through the bouncing undergrowth to find secret corners and hidden routes. Let your inner adventurer go with every step you take on this exciting journey. From swaying vines to amiable creatures, each bounce offers a fresh experience with never-ending thrills.

ROBOT RIDE

Experience the excitement of speeding by whirling galaxies and dazzling lights while being driven by innovation. Discover fearless new planets and amiable robots at every turn, which will pique your curiosity and excite you. Every moment is a plunge into the unknown, when dreams and realities collide, whether it be elegant cityscapes or far-off worlds. So fasten your seatbelt and let the Robot Ride take you on an exciting journey through the future.

TOP SPIN

Prepare to be twisted and flipped in all directions while you twist and turn, defying gravity every step of the way. As the platform tilts and whirls, taking you on a heart-pounding experience, feel the rush of excitement. Feel the rush of air on your skin and the thrill of weightlessness with every turn. See glimpses of the world below, a whirl of lights and laughter, among the mayhem. The Top Spin offers nonstop thrill that has you gasping for air and itching for more, from breathtaking heights to dangerous plummet.

MAYUR TRAIN

The Mayur Train invites travelers to set off on a fantastical trip through colorful scenery and mouthwatering cultural attractions. Board the elaborately decorated carriages that have elaborate decorations and peacock feathers. Enjoy the vivid colors of marine life that shimmer below the surface, displaying the dynamism and thrill.

FISH ROLLER COASTER RIDE

On the Fish Roller Coaster, engage in an exhilarating voyage and have an unforgettable adventure. Put on your seatbelt and head into the chasm while you travel through a vibrant environment. As you continue on this thrilling adventure, feel the excitement of the gentle breeze and the adrenaline it gives you. Select a beloved fish from an array of vibrant hues and go on an unforgettable journey with them.

CATTER PILLAR TRAIN

Experience the cool mist by hoping on our caterpillar train as it navigates over twisting slides and gushing waterfalls, and listen to other travelers laughing. Sail over soaring palm fingers and wacky artwork to lose yourself in a vibrant aquatic fantasy. Discover the thrill of adventure and the delight of discovery at every turn. Come along on a journey on the Caterpillar Train that is sure to amuse and delight you at every turn.

DRAGON PILLAR

Hop aboard the Dragon Adventure Ride for a magical journey! Little ones giggle with delight as they zip through enchanting forests and mystical valleys. Friendly dragons greet them with roars of excitement, whisking them away on a journey of wonder. With colorful carriages and playful tunes, it's a ride that sparks imagination and joy in every child's heart.

CAT TRAIN

Step onto the modern, cat-inspired carriages and experience a thrilling journey. As the feline prowls over twisting paths and exhilarating turns, taking riders on a fascinating adventure, feel its lively personality. As passengers navigate the unique bends and turns of the cat-shaped train, you can hear their happy laughing. Come along for a purr-fectly enjoyable journey on the Cat Train, where there will always be fun and feline enchantment.

BATTERY CAR

Discover aquatic wonders on the Battery Car Ride, a thrilling kid-focused water park adventure. Hope on the playful electric car and speed down the wet paths while laughing and making splashes. Experience this aquatic wonderland's swirls, twists, and unexpected water jets as you ride through it. Young explorers find unexpected surprises and hidden riches at every corner. The Battery Car Ride ensures a ton of excitement and lifelong memories whether you race alone or with friends.

MINI COASTER

Your heart will race with each curve as you zip and speed through the twists and turns, feeling the adrenaline. Feel the thrill of a full-size coaster in smaller form with every plunge. The Mini Coaster is ideal for young thrill-seekers and coaster fans alike since it offers a lot of excitement in a compact design, making the ride an unforgettable experience for everyone brave enough to take it.

Jet Plane

Take a thrilling ride on the Rocket Ride, where every climb promises adventure. As you get ready for takeoff, fasten your seatbelt and feel the excitement rising with each countdown. Take off into the skies with a loud bang, driven by your creativity and inventiveness. Defy gravity and feel weightless as you soar past vast galaxies and sparkling stars. The Rocket Ride offers an incredible adventure full of thrills, laughter, and memories that will take you to new heights of excitement.

FAMILY TRAIN

Experience a watery journey for all ages as you dive into family fun on the Family Train! Take to the exciting rafts and go on a meandering voyage through lanes brimming with aqua. Feel the refreshing spray of water as you hope in our exciting family train. Share joy and laughter at every opportunity to make priceless memories with one another. As you explore the aquatic delights of the water park, you can hear the happy squeals of children and the soft hum of the train.

Baby Rides

Take a fanciful voyage on the Animal Oasis, where your favorite animals appear as water rides. Select from a variety of animal-shaped boats, each providing a distinctive experience. Enter the animal realm and feel the wonder with every spray and splash. Every trip is an exciting journey filled with surprises and cute rides. Let the Animal Oasis serve as your entryway to a world of wonders and never-ending enjoyment.

PIGGY RACING TRACK

Get ready for an exciting ride at the Piggy Racing Track, where cheers and oinks blend together in a whirlwind of pleasure! As the determined pigs sprint to the finish line with their trotters, assemble around the track. Hear the cheers of the audience as these chubby racers whip around bends and obstacles, with each snouty rival fighting for the top spot. Feel the excitement of the chase and the delight of friendly rivalry with every squeal and snort.

DINOSAUR TRAIN

Discover thrills at the water park with the Dinosaur Train, a thrilling journey that combines aquatic pleasure and prehistoric thrills! Take a ride in the carriages decorated with dinosaurs and explore foggy tunnels and beautiful, water-filled rainforests. Experience the mist of cold waterfalls and realistic dinosaur models that lurk around every corner. For explorers of all ages, the sound of powerful roars echoing and fun water features splashing creates an immersive experience.

Coffee Cup

Savor a thrilling ride through the water on the Coffee Cup Ride, a distinctive feature of the water park! Splash and spray water all about you as you climb into giant coffee mugs and go into a caffeine-fueled frenzy. Experience an exhilarating burst of energy as you spin and swirl through water currents, with every turn feeling like you're stirring your best brew.

Balloon Umbrella

You'll softly glide far above the water as the vibrant umbrella lifts you into the air. Enjoy a safe and thrilling journey while taking in the expansive vistas and the invigorating wind. Our balloon umbrella trip is ideal for all ages and provides both thrill-seekers and families with an amazing experience. Our top goal is safety, which is why we have modern equipment and well-trained personnel.

Space plane

Take your kids on an amusing journey of fun and excitement with our newly installed space plane ride in the amusement park. Witness your kid's sunny smile and shine of joy in his laughter as he rides on this thrilling ride designed especially for munchkins who love to indulge in thrill. Bring your kids to the abode of enjoyment today.

DJ Floor

The DJ Floor is the vibrant center of the water park, so be ready to dance and have fun! Enter the dance floor, where the sound of the water's splashing mingles with the beat of the music, all surrounded by sparkling pools and bright lights. As you dance to the hottest tunes selected by the resident DJ, feel the crowd's energy pulse. Laughter and excitement fill the air as you lose yourself in a world of music and joy with every step.
