Nature Conscious Fun

As a member of the World Water Park Association, USA. the Fun N Food Water & Amusement Park offers India’s largest collection of Water slides and attractions for the toddler learning how to swim & to the Water sports enthusiast seeking a thrill. Thrills, chills, and spills don’t miss them at the Best Water Park In Delhi. We offer our patrons a new twisting turning and sliding experience.

The Fun ‘N’ Food Water & Amusement Park, located just three kilometers from the International airport on the Old Delhi Gurgaon Road. The Fun ‘N’ Food Water & Amusement Park is an endeavor to create a fusion of our rich Indian legacy and modern recreational facilities.
